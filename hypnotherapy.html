Hypnotherapy

A gentle, non-invasive approach

Hypnotherapy is utilized in a calm, non-directive manner. It does not involve:

  • Loss of control
  • Uncovering memories
  • Forced emotional processing

Instead, hypnotherapy supports:

  • Relaxation
  • Grounding
  • Nervous system settling

What sessions are like

Sessions are quiet and steady, often including simple grounding techniques, gentle guided imagery, and support with settling. Clients are reminded that sessions can pause or stop at any point, with no requirement to disclose personal information.

Working alongside other care

Hypnotherapy works alongside existing services in a collaborative and respectful way, aligned with safeguarding principles.
